Pride 2025: The Best Custom Baby Tees to Slay Your Pride Look

July 14, 2025 • 5 min read

Pride 2025 is your time to shine, and nothing says "unapologetically me" like a bold custom baby tee from Custom Baby Tee's. Whether you're strutting through a Pride parade, dancing at a queer festival, or serving looks at a rooftop party, our sassy crop tops are here to make your Pride moment iconic. Starting at just $19.95, here’s how to slay your Pride look with our tees.

Kick off with our fan-favorite “Slut” baby tee, perfect for reclaiming the narrative with pride and confidence. Pair it with rainbow fishnet tights and chunky boots for a fierce, gender-neutral look. Add a glittery fanny pack and heart-shaped sunglasses to channel full-on Pride energy. Want to make it personal? Customize it with your pronouns or a cheeky slogan like “Love Wins, Always.”

For a playful vibe, rock the “I Met Your Dad on Hinge” tee, but swap it for “I Met Your Man on Grindr” for that extra queer flair. Style it with high-waisted cargo pants in a bold color like electric blue or hot pink, and throw on a metallic bomber jacket for a look that pops in every Pride Instagram post. Our design tool lets you tweak the font or add rainbow accents in just a few clicks, with delivery in 3-5 days after approval.

Planning a group look for your chosen family? Go for matching tees with slogans like “Pride Squad” or “Born This Way” in vibrant neon colors. Pair with denim cutoffs and statement earrings for the girls, or sequined shorts and platform sneakers for the boys. Add a custom touch with each person’s name or a nod to queer icons like Lady Gaga or RuPaul. Check out our shop for inspiration, and vibe to our Pride 2025 playlist featuring bops like “Vogue” by Madonna and “Espresso” by Sabrina Carpenter to get in the mood.
